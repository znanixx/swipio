Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Swipio free?

    Swipio is free to download and use with core features. Optional premium features may be available through in-app purchases. You can start learning vocabulary right away at no cost.

  • How do I restore my subscription?

    Your subscription is tied to your App Store or Google Play account. After reinstalling the app or switching to a new device, sign in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used to subscribe-your subscription will be restored automatically. No extra steps or restore button are needed.

  • Can I transfer my subscription from iOS to Android (or the other way)?

    No. Subscriptions are tied to either the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) and don’t transfer between platforms. You’d need to subscribe again on the new OS. On a new device with the same platform (e.g. a new iPhone), sign in with the same account and your subscription will be restored automatically.

  • Notifications don’t work or arrive at the wrong time

    First, ensure notifications are enabled in Swipio’s settings. Then check your device’s battery or power-saving settings - these vary by manufacturer, and aggressive power-saving modes can block or delay local push notifications.

  • Does Swipio work offline?

    Yes. Once you have downloaded your word sets, you can study offline. You only need an internet connection for initial setup, downloading new content, and optional features like syncing or audio.
